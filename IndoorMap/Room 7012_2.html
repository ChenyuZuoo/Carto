Room 0712

Room 0712 is a lecture room with 12 computers. The lecture room can be used for lectures that needs the support of a computer. For example when students need to learn to work with programs like ArcGIS or Adobe Photoshop. The computers are equipped with the latest software and a lot of different programs. The room offers places for 24 students. Next to the lectures, the room can also be used as a place to study. Therefore the schedule need to be consulted, to check the reservation for lectures.
